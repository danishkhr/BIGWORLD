About BIGWORLD

What is BIGWORLD?

BIGWORLD 2025 is an annual event organized by the Finance and Banking Society (FABS) of Universiti Utara Malaysia (UUM). It's more than just a competition — it's a dynamic platform for future business leaders to challenge themselves through interactive workshops, real-world simulations, networking sessions, and team-based challenges. Through joining BIGWORLD 2025, participants can get golden opportunities to showcase their knowledge as well as expertise in finance and banking related worlds.

Background

BIGWORLD is a competition-based event organised by Finance and Banking Society (FABS) that comprises team-based competition event covers the finance and banking area which focus on some themes to let participants to apply their knowledge in the event.

There are some highlighted events throughout the event, such as speakers from various industries will share their experiences, solutions, and best practices in the focused areas.

Our Objectives

  • Enhance the application of financial and banking knowledge in real-world scenarios.
  • Connect participants with the financial and banking industry partners in the social world.
  • Empower participants in personal and professional development.
